# HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)
# Project Objective
This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze employee data and understand key factors influencing attrition, workforce distribution, and organizational performance.

## Dataset

### The dataset used for this project contains HR-related information such as employee demographics, salary, job roles, education, and attrition details.

### Download Dataset:
-<a href="https://github.com/yourusername/HR-Analytics-Dashboard/blob/main/HR_Analytics_Dataset.csv">Dataset</a>
##Key Features

• Overall Workforce Overview
o Total Employees: 63
o Attrition Count: 12
o Attrition Rate: 19.0%

• Employee Metrics
o Average Age: 38 years
o Average Salary: 6.7K
o Average Experience: 7 years

• Attrition by Gender
o Female: 6
o Male: 5

• Attrition by Education

Distribution across:

 Human Resources (58%)
 Technical Degree (17%)
 Medical (17%)

• Attrition by Age Group
o Highest attrition observed in 26–35 age group
o Moderate attrition in 18–25 and 36–45

• Attrition by Salary
o Majority attrition in employees earning up to 5K
o Minimal attrition in higher salary ranges

• Attrition by Years at Company
o Highest attrition in early years (0–3 years)
o Decreases as experience increases

• Attrition by Job Role
o Human Resources shows highest attrition (12)
o Manager role shows minimal/no attrition

• Interactive Filters
o Department-wise filtering:
 Human Resources
 Research & Development
 Sales
 ## Tools & Technologies Used

• Data Modeling & Data Cleaning
